Behind the Lens

I'm Rich — known as Akwaman since high school, when a teacher heard my last name and the whole class lit up. That story of something unexpected becoming something lasting is exactly how I'd describe my relationship with photography.

I shoot weddings, events, sports, and life — the planned and the spontaneous. Based in Drexel Hill, PA, I've taken my camera everywhere from the waterfront of Savannah to Ghana and back.

Every shot is a chance to freeze something real. I take that seriously — and I have a lot of fun doing it. Picture your memories.
